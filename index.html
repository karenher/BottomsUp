BottomsUp

Do you want to be healthy but need an extra push?


BottomsUp is a cushion you can sit on that will remind you when you have been sitting down for too long. It will send a text to your phone to help you be healthy.

We all want to live long, healthy lives, but...

over 50 percent of American men, and 60 percent of American women, never engage in any vigorous physical activity lasting more than 10 minutes per week. Exercise deficiency threatens both physical and mental wellness, shortening your lifespan.

Sitting down can increase heart failure.

Experiments show that sitting down for too long can be fatal for your health. Those who sat for the long periods of time were twice as likely to have diabetes or heart disease.

So, stand up!

The mere act of standing up from a seated position is effective at counteracting the detrimental health effects of sitting. Standing up four times an hour can significantly increase your health. Most people will forget to do this once working, and ButtUp is here to help.
